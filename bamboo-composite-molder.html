Bamboo Composite Molder

Bamboo production is one of the emerging sectors of the country in terms of socioeconomic value and environmental sustainability. Its versatility in product design and its sustainability from production to end-of-life has become popular in the local and foreign markets. However, challenges remain in the full utilization of bamboo and its potential as a sustainable material. During the post-processing of bamboo products, 33.5% to 70% of bamboo ends up as waste.

One solution to this problem is utilizing bamboo wastes in biocomposite materials. However, the technology developed for bamboo-based biocomposite processing is only available in large industries abroad.

Project Leader: Engr. Dan William C. Martinez, MSME
