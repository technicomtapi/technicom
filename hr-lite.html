HR Lite

Based on the experience of deploying the system through a project-based contract, customizing, tailor-fitting, configuring, and transitioning from old to current system entails too much work, time, and money. Hence, the DOST-ASTI ERP researchers have selected the highly utilized regular processes, required modules and documents in accordance with Philippine government set of rules and guidelines.

This data will be used in creating the “HR Lite”, an improved version of the ERP designed for easy configuration and with focused sets of functionalities to cater the most basic and common administrative processes of the government. It should be light enough to be installed easily, and it can be configured and enhanced according to the client’s needs in the future.

Project Leader: Paul John M. Serrano
