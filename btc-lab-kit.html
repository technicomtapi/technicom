BTC Lab Kit

We propose to develop a teaching kit particularly by providing a Built Your Own Tissue Culture Kit (BTC Lab Kit) that would help teachers teach and students learn the concepts in the course.

The kit is designed too introduce practical learning of concepts and integration of information from Junior High School courses in this spiral curriculum.

Project Leader: Dr. Ma. Carmen Ablan Lagma,
