Project Geomap

This research undertaking is found to be significant primarily due to the following reasons:

(1) the outcome of this r&d will increase crops production in the local municipalities that are covered by this undertaking;

(2) the farmers will be guided as to what crops to be cultivated on their particular agricultural land based on its soil profile and characteristics which at the end increase the volume of productions and crop yields;

(3) concerned government agencies can monitor crops and yield production and farmers relevant information which include farming activities, technologies and techniques for their immediate intervention when necessary; and

(4) the outcome of the study specially the expert system will greatly help the crops production and farming activities of the farmers and that of the concerned government agencies.

Project Leader: Engr. Raven C. Tabiongan
Samar State University
