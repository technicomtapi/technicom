IOT - Based Monitoring System

The machine shop monitoring system has a feature to determine machine utilization which is an important performance parameter of a manufacturing facility. By measuring machine utilization, production can be increased by making better data-driven decisions and by reducing the risk of unplanned downtime.

There are commercially available monitoring systems in the market abroad but they are costly and require support from experts outside the country. The localization of this technology can potentially lessen the initial investment cost of local machine shops.
