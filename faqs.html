  • The Program shall provide priority funding to pre-commercialization activities of projects aligned to national S&T priorities and project proposal’s strategic relevance to national development, based on the priority areas of the DOST’s Harmonized National R&D Agenda (HNRDA) for 2022-2028. Some of these areas are the following: Agriculture, Aquatic, and Natural Resources Crops Livestock Aquatic Forestry Natural resources and environment Climate change adaptation and mitigation and disaster risk reduction Health* Drug discovery and development Functional Food Re-emerging and Emerging Diseases Diagnostics Omic technologies for health (platform technology across research priorities) Biomedical devices engineering for health Digital and frontier health technologies Disaster risk reduction and climate change adaptation in health Mental health *NOTE: To be qualified under the Program and to ensure safety and effectiveness, for pharmaceuticals the technology must be at TRL 7 (completed at least Phase II trials). For medical devices, the technology must be at TRL 6.
